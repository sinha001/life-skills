## 2. Introduction to Growth Mindset

### 2. Paraphrase (Summarize) the video in a few (1 or 2) lines.

- The video explains that people with a fixed mindset that their skills can't change and avoid feedback, while those with a growth mindset learn from mistakes, accept feedback, and keep improving.

---

## 3. Understanding Internal Locus of Control

### 3. What is the Internal Locus of Control? What is the key point in the video?

Internal Locus of Control is the belief that your own actions, decisions, and efforts determine the outcomes in your life.

The Key Points are:

- Take responsibility for their results.

- Work hard and stay motivated.

- Believe they can influence outcomes.

- Do not blame luck or external factors.


---
## 4. How to build a Growth Mindset

### 4. What are the key points mentioned by speaker to build growth mindset?

- I believe “I can figure it out” — trust that I can learn or improve.

- I question my own assumptions (not accepting things as fixed).

- I develop my own “life curriculum” — learning continuously and on my terms.

- I accept challenges.
 
- I learn from failure and mistakes.

- I use feedback to improve.

- I focus on effort and persistence.

- I commit to lifelong learning.

## 5. Mindset - A MountBlue Warrior Reference Manual

### 5. What are your ideas to take action and build Growth Mindset?

- Take challenges as opportunities to learn.

- Learn from failures and mistakes.

- Use feedback and criticism to improve.

- Focus on effort and persistence, not just talent.

- Keep learning new skills and stay curious.

- Practise positive self-talk and believe in your ability to grow.

- Surround yourself with growth-minded people.

- Set realistic but challenging goals to guide your learning.

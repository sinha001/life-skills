# Focus Management

## 1. What is Deep Work?

Deep Work means focusing on one important task without any distractions.
No phone, no multitasking, no noise — just full concentration.
This helps you learn faster and do better work.

## 2. According to author how to do deep work properly, in a few points?

- Find a quiet place to work.
- Turn off your phone and notifications.
- Decide exactly what you want to work on.
- Work at the same time every day so it becomes a habit.
- Keep shallow work (emails, messages) for later.

## 3. How can you implement the principles in your day to day life?

- Set a daily “focus time” (even 30–45 minutes).
- Put your phone in another room.
- Work on only one task at a time.
- Tell people not to disturb you during this time.
- Take short breaks after your focus session.

## 4. What are the dangers of social media, in brief?

- It breaks your focus.
- It wastes a lot of time.
- It makes your attention span shorter.
- It can cause stress, anxiety, or low confidence.
- It makes you compare your life with others.
